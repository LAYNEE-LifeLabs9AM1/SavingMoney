Our website generally talks about saving money. The content specifically tackles how to save money practically in easy ways and the benefits of saving money that will surely be beneficial for people that want to save money.

Live a Simple Life

Live simple is an advice given by Warren Buffet, one of today’s wealthiest billionaires today with an estimated net worth of $88 Billion. Although he could buy himself luxurious and fancy cars or buy today’s expensive, high tech smartphones, Mr. Buffet keeps it simple by using flip phones and using simple cars.
